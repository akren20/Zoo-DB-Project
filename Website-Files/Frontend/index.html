Welcome To The University of Houston Zoo

Where anything (within reason) is possible! Nestled in the heart of Houston, our Zoo offers an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages. Get ready to embark on a wild adventure as you encounter fascinating creatures from around the globe.

Things To Do At The Zoo!

At the University of Houston Zoo, visitors can embark on a captivating journey through a diverse array of wildlife habitats and engaging exhibits. From the majestic big cats prowling in their enclosures to the playful primates swinging through the trees, there's something to captivate every animal lover. Visitors can participate in educational programs led by knowledgeable staff, offering insights into conservation efforts and the importance of biodiversity. Families can enjoy interactive experiences such as feeding sessions and behind-the-scenes tours, providing an up-close encounter with some of the zoo's most fascinating inhabitants. Additionally, the zoo offers picturesque picnic areas and scenic walking trails, inviting guests to relax and soak in the natural beauty of their surroundings. Whether it's a day of learning, exploration, or simply enjoying the wonders of the animal kingdom, the University of Houston Zoo promises an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages.

Amenities

Visitors can enjoy a range of amenities designed to enhance their experience. These include convenient parking facilities, wheelchair accessibility, and clean restroom facilities throughout the park. Guests can also refuel at on-site cafes and snack bars offering a variety of refreshments. For those seeking souvenirs or gifts, the zoo's gift shop provides a selection of merchandise featuring their favorite animals. With these amenities, guests can comfortably explore and enjoy all that the University of Houston Zoo has to offer.

Exhibits

You can embark on an immersive journey through a variety of captivating exhibits showcasing diverse wildlife from around the globe. From the lush rainforests to the vast savannahs, each exhibit transports guests to different ecosystems, providing glimpses into the lives of fascinating animals. With engaging displays and informative signage, guests can explore, learn, and connect with the wonders of the natural world at the University of Houston Zoo.

Tours

Our tours offer a deeper understanding of the zoo's inhabitants, conservation efforts, and the importance of biodiversity. Guests can choose from options such as behind-the-scenes tours, where they can explore areas not typically open to the public and learn about the zoo's operations firsthand. There are also themed tours focusing on specific animal species or habitats, providing fascinating insights into their behaviors and adaptations.
