Get your tickets here!

Gold

Early Admission: Gold Tier - Early admission allows members to enter the zoo before regular opening hours, providing exclusive access to the exhibits and attractions when the zoo is less crowded, enhancing the overall experience.

Priority Booking: Gold Tier - Members receive priority booking for popular zoo programs, events, or experiences, ensuring they have the opportunity to secure their spots ahead of the general public, maximizing their enjoyment of the zoo.

VIP Areas: Gold Tier - Access to VIP lounges or viewing areas where members can relax in comfort and enjoy prime views of animal exhibits, providing an elevated and luxurious zoo experience.

Complimentary Souvenir: Gold Tier - Members receive a special complimentary souvenir upon joining or renewing their membership, serving as a token of appreciation and a memento of their commitment to the zoo.

Free Parking: Gold Tier - Members benefit from complimentary parking at the zoo, saving on parking fees with each visit, and making their trips more convenient and cost-effective.

Gold Tier - Members receive up to three food vouchers redeemable at select food outlets within the zoo, providing them with complimentary meals or snacks during their visits, enhancing their dining experience.

Silver

Free Parking: Silver Tier - Members benefit from complimentary parking at the zoo, saving on parking fees with each visit, and making their trips more convenient and cost-effective.

1 Food Voucher: Silver Tier - Members receive one food voucher redeemable at select food outlets within the zoo, providing them with a complimentary meal or snack during their visit, enhancing their dining experience.

Early Admission: Silver Tier - Members enjoy exclusive early admission to the zoo, allowing them to explore the exhibits and attractions before the general public, providing a tranquil and immersive experience.

Priority Booking: Silver Tier - Members receive priority booking for popular zoo programs, events, or experiences, ensuring they have the opportunity to secure their spots ahead of the general public, maximizing their enjoyment of the zoo.

Bronze

Early Admission: Bronze Tier - Members enjoy early admission to the zoo, allowing them to beat the crowds and explore the exhibits and attractions before the general public, providing a peaceful and enjoyable experience.

Priority Booking: Bronze Tier - Members receive priority booking for popular zoo programs, events, or experiences, ensuring they have the opportunity to secure their spots ahead of the general public, maximizing their enjoyment of the zoo.

Free Parking: Bronze Tier - Members benefit from complimentary parking at the zoo, saving on parking fees with each visit, and making their trips more convenient and cost-effective.
